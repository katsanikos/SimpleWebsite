News

September 2009

On 10 September 2009, following an Internet campaign, British Prime Minister Gordon Brown made an official public apology on behalf of the British government for "the appalling way he was treated."

December 2013

The Queen granted him a posthumous pardon on 24 December 2013.

December 2014

The historical drama The Imitation Game, directed by Morten Tyldum and starring Benedict Cumberbatch as Turing, is set for cinematic release in 2014.
